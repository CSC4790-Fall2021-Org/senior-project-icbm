The ICBM Investing tool is designed to recommend a general investing strategy that is personalized for your needs. It analyzes the same broad criteria used by most financial advisors-- time horizon, investment objectives, and risk tolerance. what makes the ICBM tool unique is it's added emphasis on ESG investing (Environmental, Social, Governance) that ensures is recommendations align with your personal ethics. The ICBM tool also exclusively recommends ETFs (Exchange-Traded Funds), which are generally more stable than stocks. it is also important to note that the ICBM tool is not backed by any certified financial advice, and is intended merely as a guidance tool.
